Frequently Asked Question

You May Ask

what do you teach here?
Here at Your Trader Learning, We teach students to become professional traders. We have 4 different types of share market courses in Delhi which are specially designed to help you become a successful trader. We have made more than 2000 students as a professional trader. We do not do any kind of broker business, it is solely related to buying and selling of stocks individually.
Why should i join Stock Market Cource from YTL.
YTL is one of the most reputed stock market institutes in Delhi. We have the best faculties who have several years of experience of trading in market and teaching. We will teach you everything practically and in live market hours thus give you chance of handsome returns on your investments.
Will the cource provides me the placement?
Our courses are designed in a way that it will be fruitful for both the investors to gain advance knowledge and also for the students who are fresher and would like to make their career in the stock market. After the successful completion of our any course and getting certifications, students will be easily able to get job or placement in any broking house of India. We will provide you complete assistance in getting the job.

How much money I can expect from stock market?
You can expect up to 10% per month of your investments in the stock market but that all depends as how much you are investing, your portfolio and also how you are using the strategies taught by us in institute.
How do i submit fee? Can i pay fees in installments?
You can make the payment either by transferring the funds online to our company account or you can swipe the card. In some cases, you can make the payment in cash. Yes, we give the facility to our students to pay in Instalments.
