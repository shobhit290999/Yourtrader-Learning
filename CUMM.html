Currency Market Module

Currency market module is a one month programme in NISM series I Certification. The NISM currency derivative certification exam is known to be the toughest exam. YTL Institute prepares students for NISM currency market examination- mandated by SEBI to get a job in Indian Currency Market. It is an entry-level program for aspirants willing to work in Indian Stock Exchange.


The currency market has evolved during the last years. Perhaps, witness a distinct growth in the last few years. The advent in the currency market is accompanied by modernization and commercialization of investment banking and globalization of financial activities. The NISM Currency certification examination aims to create in-depth knowledge for working professionals in the currency derivatives sector. The course delivers a deep understanding of operational processes and risk management. The NISM Derivatives examination is a standard exam for traders working in the currency market.


Currency market program gives comprehensive knowledge on the fundamental tools. Also, learn the role of demand and supply mechanism, impact of government policies, and the effect of macroeconomic indicators. Thus, the currency market certification program aims to reduce uncertainties to losses using the fundamental analytical framework.
